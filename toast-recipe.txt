1 Ingredients:
 - 2 pieces sliced bread (white, wheat, sourdough, rhye, etc)
 - 1 tbsp spreadable or whipped butter

Directions:
 1. Put bread in toaster
 2. Set toaster to brown (about numer 3 on my toaster dial)
 3. When toast pops up, spread butter on each piece
 4. Serve on plate, or just stuff in face over sink
